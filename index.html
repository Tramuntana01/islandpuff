Responsible Consumption

Responsible consumption is essential for safe and legal enjoyment. Here are some best practices:

Know Your Body

Start with small doses and gradually increase according to your tolerance. Everyone is different, find your optimal level.

Choose the Moment

Consume in safe and comfortable environments. Avoid driving or performing activities that require concentration.

Surround Yourself with Trust

Share the experience only with trusted people. A good environment is essential for responsible enjoyment.

🏥 Stay Informed

Learn about the product you consume. Know the origin, potency and expected effects.

🚫 Respect Limits

You are not obligated to consume just because others do. Your health and well-being are priorities.

💪 Take Care of Your Health

If you have medical conditions, consult a professional. Responsible consumption respects your health.

⚠️ Important Warnings

It is crucial to be informed about the risks associated with cannabis consumption:

Minors

Cannabis is prohibited for minors under 18. The brain continues developing until age 25, and consumption can affect this process.

Pregnancy and Lactation

Consumption is not recommended during pregnancy or lactation. It can affect fetal development and pass into the baby.

Driving

Never drive under the effects of cannabis. It affects reflexes and reaction capacity. Your safety is first!

Health Mental

Cannabis can trigger or worsen mental health problems. If you have a history of schizophrenia or psychosis, consult a specialist.

Dependency

Although less addictive than other substances, frequent use can create psychological dependence. Consume with moderation.

Legality

Cannabis is only legal in private associative spaces. Do not transport it on the street or consume it in public places.
